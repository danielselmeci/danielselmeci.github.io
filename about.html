Biography

Daniel Selmeci is a tech entrepreneur, award-winning photographer, and content creator who builds digital tools to enhance the modern travel experience. He is the founder of Polymath Sphere LLC, a venture dedicated to creative and technical innovation, and the creator of Go: TravelApp, a platform designed to streamline journey planning and exploration for global travelers.

Driven by a profound passion for exploration, Daniel has heavily documented his international journeys through a portfolio focused on nature, architecture, and portraiture. His distinctive visual storytelling earned him recognition as a finalist in Shoot The Wild, an international wild photography award. His photograph Castle in the forest was also featured by Shoot The Frame as work by community member Daniel Selmeci. Utilizing advanced gear like the Nikon D780 and the DJI Mavic 2 Pro, he captures unique aerial and ground perspectives from major global hubs, ranging from historical landmarks in Rome to the urban landscape of New York City.

As a high-volume international traveler, Daniel directly applies his firsthand global insights into the development of Go: TravelApp. By merging his technical expertise as an entrepreneur with his practical knowledge as an explorer, he focuses on creating seamless digital solutions that connect people to new destinations.
