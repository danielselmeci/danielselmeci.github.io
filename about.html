Biography

Daniel Selmeci is a tech entrepreneur, award-winning photographer, and content creator who builds digital tools to enhance the modern travel experience. He is the founder of Polymath Sphere LLC, a venture dedicated to creative and technical innovation, and the creator of Go: Travel App, a platform designed to streamline journey planning and exploration for global travelers.

Driven by a profound passion for exploration, Daniel has heavily documented his international journeys through a portfolio focused on nature, architecture, and portraiture. His distinctive visual storytelling earned him recognition as a finalist in the international Shoot The Frame photography awards. Utilizing advanced gear like the Nikon D780 and the DJI Mavic 2 Pro, he captures unique aerial and ground perspectives from major global hubs, ranging from historical landmarks in Rome to the urban landscape of New York City.

As a high-volume international traveler, Daniel directly applies his firsthand global insights into the development of GO: Travel App. By merging his technical expertise as an entrepreneur with his practical knowledge as an explorer, he focuses on creating seamless digital solutions that connect people to new destinations.
